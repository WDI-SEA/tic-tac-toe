Tic Tac Game

Directions

The object of Tic Tac Toe is to get three in a row. You play on a three by three game board. The first player is known as X and the second is O. Players alternate placing Xs and Os on the game board until either oppenant has three in a row or all nine squares are filled.

Player 1 "X" Turn, CLICK ON A SQUARE!

Player 2 O's Turn, CLICK ON A SQUARE!

X Wins! O Wins!
