Tic-Tac-Toe

Can you beat the computer?

The year is 2036. Artificial intelligence is leaps and bounds ahead of what it once was in the 2020s, and as a result, robots live alongside human beings, many of whom are cyborgs with biomechatronics. In this new world, Tic-Tac-Toe is the universal law. All crimes, decisions, and contests are decidided with a final game of Tic-Tac-Toe. Robots believe they are smarter than humans and go out of their way to prove it. A foolish robot has challenged you to a game, betting his brand new MetaCorp Roadster for your motorcycle. Teach this node-headed bot a lesson and take his car! Make sure to ride off into the night at speeds above 200mph.
