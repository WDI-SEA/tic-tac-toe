Ari's Game Of
TIC TAC TOE

This is a game of TIC TAC TOE: A two player game (unless you have no friends) that consists of a simple 3x3 board. To play, simply select a tile, clicking marks your tile with "X", the next player can then select the tile they want, their tile will be marked with an "O". The player who has 3 of each in a row (either vertically, horizontally, or diagonally) first wins the game! To reset the game, simply click the game title in the rectangle above.
